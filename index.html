01 — Thesis

What is  culture capital?

Money is a commodity. The best companies have their choice of capital partners — what separates winners from also-rans is the ability to command attention while filtering out the noise. Social media is the dominant exchange of information today, and our portfolio is built around founders who already know how to win it.

Seed-Stage, In-Revenue

We invest early — but in companies that have already proven they can generate revenue. Not pre-product, not pre-customer.

Inter-Portfolio Engagement

Every company in our portfolio has been introduced to the others. We treat the portfolio as a network, not a spreadsheet — actively engineering collaboration where it creates value.

Optics on the Next Round

Through advisory positions and regular engagement, we maintain visibility into each company's next injection of capital and ultimate liquidity event.
